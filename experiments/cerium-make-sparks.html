Observe sparks. safety_description: | Wear eye protection goggles. science_background: | Cerium powder is formed by friction of the sandpaper. The particulate cerium is pyrophoric, it ignites spontaneously in air. The contact surface of the metal with oxygen increases during grinding. Cerium atoms located at the surface of the particle combine with oxygen in the air and are oxidized. The reaction is exothermic, some of the energy is transmitted to the neighboring particles of cerium, they pass into the excited state, and the reaction rate with oxygen increases.
