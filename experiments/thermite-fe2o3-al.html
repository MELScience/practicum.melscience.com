Maximum protection the place of the reaction. science_background: | Thermite is widely used in both civil and military purposes. Aluminum is a more active metal than iron and stands in the electrochemical series of metals activity more left. Due to this fact, aluminum may reduce iron from iron compounds, including oxide, and forms a more stable compound – aluminium oxide. The difference in the strength of the bond Fe-O and Al-O is so great that a large amount of heat is released during the reaction, which can melt the resulting iron. In turn, iron plates are destroyed by the melt.
