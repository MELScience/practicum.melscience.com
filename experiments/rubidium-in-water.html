When it comes in contact with water it reacts rapidly as follows: 2Rb + H2O --> 2RbOH + H2 A lot of heat is produced in this reaction. It passes faster than the same reaction of other alkaline metals (except for francium and cesium) because the alkaline properties increase with size of atom.
