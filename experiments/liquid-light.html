Put 0.2 g of luminol, 12 g of sodium bicarbonate and 0.5 g of copper sulfate into a beaker. 2. Pour about 500 ml of water and add a few drops of ammonia solution. Luminol dissolves better in an alkaline solution (you may also use sodium hydroxide to alkalize it). Stir to dissolve all compounds. 3. Prepare the second solution - 5 ml of hydrogen peroxide in 500 ml of water in another beaker. 4. Mix the same amounts of these solutions to obtain chemiluminescence. You may mix solutions in different ways - in a beaker, in a long tube, make a fountain in a flask with decreased pressure and so on. safety_description: | Wear eye protection and lab gloves.
