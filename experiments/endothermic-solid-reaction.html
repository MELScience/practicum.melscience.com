It should be about -25 degrees Celsius. safety_description: | Wear eye protection goggles. science_background: | During this reaction, the two solids form a liquid and a gas. Thus, the temperature of the reaction can be positive or negative. The thermal effect of the reaction can be calculated based on the standard enthalpies of formation of the materials.
