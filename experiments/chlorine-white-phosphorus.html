Chlorine is a poisonous gas! Perform the reaction in a hood or outdoors. science_background: | As a halogen chlorine is a strong oxidant. This property of chlorine is due to its position in the periodic table, high electronegativity, the lack of a single electron to fill the outer electron level and the low energy of Cl-Cl bonds. Phosphorus is a nonmetal that has reducing properties. It, especially white phosphorus, has a very high reactivity. Phosphorus is oxidized to phosphorus chloride, which forms the smoke.
